China - Tangshan Industrial Heat Source Display Platform
Please note: This webpage will continue to be updated. Stay tuned for future announcements.
1.Tangshan has many large steel plants, chemical plants, and power plants, in addition to numerous small factories and mining enterprises, which serve as long-term heat sources. These heat sources can cause pollution to the surrounding air and water quality, and may even affect the production, living conditions, and health of nearby residents. By monitoring the heat emissions from these industrial and mining enterprises, the distribution of these heat sources can be clearly observed. By monitoring these heat sources using satellite data, there is great potential to estimate emission inventories. This crucial information can guide environmental protection departments in designing effective emission reduction measures and ultimately enhancing regional environmental quality.
